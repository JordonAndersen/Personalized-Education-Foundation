About Personalized Education Foundation

At the Personalized Education Foundation, we are on a mission to transform lives through education. Our goal is to empower students and families by providing access to a personalized education experience.

We achieve this through fundraising, Tuition Assistance, and the creation of an endowment for current and prospective members of the Christa McAuliffe Academy School of Arts and Sciences (CMASAS) community.

With your support, we're making K-12 and college educational opportunities more affordable for those where a private education is just out of reach. The Personalized Education Foundation is a 501(c)(3) organization, so all donations are tax-deductible.

CMASAS School History

Inspired by the heroic life and acting in her memory, CMASAS honors Christa McAuliffe by educating the next generation of global leaders. CMASAS opened in July 2009 and is the culmination of over 25 years of combined experience, knowledge, and perseverance in alternative education of its Co-founder and Executive Director Christopher Geis.

Since 2009, CMASAS has grown from its rather humble beginnings into a thriving worldwide organization headquartered in the Pacific Northwest. CMASAS operates under Personalized Education Group, Inc., an educational management organization. Our knowledgeable, experienced, and passionate team of administrators, office staff, and educators foster a sense of community and an atmosphere of harmony where new ideas can take root and grow more easily and where innovation is welcomed and applauded.

Our Kauai Education Partnership

Bringing personalized education opportunities to the Garden Isle

Expanding Educational Access in Kauai

Our partnership with Kauai educators and community leaders is bringing personalized learning opportunities to students across the island. Recognizing the unique challenges faced by families in Hawaii's more remote communities, we've established a special initiative to support Kauai students.

Through this partnership, we've already provided tuition assistance to 42 Kauai students, helping them access CMASAS's innovative educational model without leaving their island home.

Our Kauai-specific programs include:

  • Localized curriculum connections to Hawaiian culture and environment
  • Specialized support for students in rural areas with limited school options
  • Community-based learning opportunities that connect classroom concepts to island life
  • Increased tuition assistance specifically for Kauai families
